About Us

   Preferred Regulatory Consulting provides regulatory, quality, and clinical services to medical device and pharmaceutical companies at all stages of their development. We assist with products in all three medical device classes, including software products, active implantable devices, combination products as well as pharmaceutical products.
  Our experience is in the areas of cardiovascular, respiratory, ENT, opthalmology, gastroenterology, urology, nephrology, women's health, neurology/neurodegenerative, orthopedics and metabolic diseases. Our consultants bring decades of experience in regulatory, clinical and quality management at strategic and operational level.
  The practice is led by Dr. Plamena Entcheva-Dimitrov, who founded the firm in 2011.
