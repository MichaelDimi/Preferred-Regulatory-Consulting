Training

Training is essential in a regulated industry such as the healthcare industry. Participants of all levels in the industry and academia could take advantage of on-line training at their convenience. Often times, regulatory affairs, quality systems and GCP training is provided by universities and professional groups and it is designed to achieve a certificate in the end.

However, a lot of professionals and academia representatives would like to educate themselves on a specific topic without the need to work toward a certificate. We have developed training to satisfy these individuals, who are eager to learn. We provide training on variety of subject in regulatory affairs, quality systems and GCP.
